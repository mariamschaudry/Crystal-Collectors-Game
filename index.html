Crystal Collectors!

How to Play:

At the start of the game, you will be given a random target number. Located below the number, there are four crystals. By clicking on a crystal will add a specified amount of points to your total score. To win the game, match your total score with the target number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random target number. The value of each crystal is hidden until clicked on. Each time the game starts, the value of the crystals will change.
